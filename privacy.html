Privacy Policy

Signo - Electronic Signature
Last updated: February 17, 2026

Signo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your signatures and documents never leave your device.

Overview

Signo ("the App") is developed by gguggulab. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Data Collection

Signo does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking data is collected
  • No signatures or documents are uploaded to any server
  • No cookies or identifiers are used
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included

How the App Works

All signature creation and document signing is performed entirely on your device. The App:

  • Creates and stores signatures locally on your device
  • Signs PDF documents using on-device processing only
  • Accesses your files, photo library, or camera only when you explicitly choose to import a document
  • Stores signed documents locally on your device
  • Does not require an internet connection to function

Files, Photo Library & Camera Access

The App requests access to your files, photo library, and camera solely for the purpose of importing documents to sign. These permissions are optional and requested only when needed. No files or images are transmitted off your device.

Data Storage

Signatures, signed PDF documents, and document history are stored locally on your device within the App's sandbox. This data is automatically removed when you delete the App.

Third-Party Services

Signo does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
