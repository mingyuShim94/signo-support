Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a signature?

Open Signo and tap "Create Signature". Draw your signature with your finger or Apple Pencil. Choose your ink color and adjust stroke width, then tap "Save".

How do I sign a PDF document?

Tap "Sign a Document" to import a PDF from Files, Camera, or Photo Library. Then tap to place your signature on the document. Drag and resize it to position it perfectly, then tap "Save".

Does Signo require an internet connection?

No. Signo works completely offline. All signing happens on your device — your documents are never uploaded to any server.

Where are my signed documents saved?

All signed documents are saved in the Docs tab, organized by date. You can view, share, or delete them anytime.

Can I have multiple signatures?

Yes. You can create and save multiple signatures for different purposes — personal, business, or initials. Set a default signature in Settings for quick access.

Is Signo really free?

Yes. Signo is completely free with no ads, no sign-up, no subscription, and no hidden fees. Every feature is available from day one.

How do I share a signed document?

After signing, tap the share icon to send your document via AirDrop, Mail, Messages, or any other app using the iOS Share Sheet.
